    About Us

    Get to know Us

    The Refugee Basketball Initiative (RBI) is a community-driven organisation dedicated to empowering refugee and host community youth in Kakuma, Kenya, through the game of basketball. We believe in the power of sports to inspire, unite, and transform lives.

    Founded to provide meaningful opportunities for young people in displacement settings, RBI offers training programs, tournaments, and mentorship initiatives that go beyond the court. Through basketball, we foster resilience, social cohesion, leadership, and personal development, helping youth build confidence and a sense of belonging.

    Our programs create safe spaces for young people to learn teamwork, discipline, and the values of sportsmanship while promoting mental and physical well-being. By integrating education, leadership training, and community engagement into our initiatives, we aim to equip participants with sk ills that extend far beyond sports, preparing them for a brighter future.

    Join us as we use basketball as a tool for empowerment, inclusion, and hope in Kakuma and beyond!

    Our Services

    Check Our Services

    Basketball Training & Development

    We provide structured basketball training for youth of all skill levels. Our experienced coaches focus on fundamental skills, teamwork, and strategy to help players grow in confidence and ability.

    Education & Scholarship Support

    Through our initiative, talented and dedicated players have access to scholarship opportunities that enable them to pursue formal education. We believe in using basketball as a bridge to academic success.

    Community Engagement & Social Inclusion

    RBI fosters unity by bringing together refugees and host communities through sports. Our tournaments, leagues, and outreach programs promote inclusion, teamwork, and social cohesion.

    Life Skills & Leadership Training

    Beyond sports, we equip youth with essential life skills such as leadership, discipline, teamwork, and resilience. Our mentorship programs help young athletes build confidence and prepare for future opportunities.

    Tournaments & Competitive Events

    We organize local and regional basketball tournaments, giving players the chance to showcase their skills, engage in friendly competition, and gain exposure to potential career opportunities.

    Mental Health & Well-Being Programs

    Sports play a critical role in mental health. We offer counselling, mentorship, and wellness programs that help players manage stress, build self-esteem, and find a sense of purpose through basketball.

    Empowerment for Girls in Sports

    We are committed to gender inclusion in sports by encouraging and supporting young girls to participate in basketball. Our programs create safe and empowering spaces for female athletes to thrive.
